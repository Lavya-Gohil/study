## ðŸ“‹ Exam Pattern

### NEET Paper Structure

**Total Questions**: 200
- Physics: 50 questions
- Chemistry: 50 questions
- Biology: 100 questions (Botany 50 + Zoology 50)

**Physics Section Structure**:
- **Section A**: 35 questions (attempt all)
- **Section B**: 15 questions (attempt any 10)
- **Total to Attempt**: 45 questions

### Marking Scheme
- **Correct Answer**: +4 marks
- **Incorrect Answer**: -1 mark
- **Unattempted**: 0 marks
- **Total Physics Marks**: 180 marks

### Exam Duration
- **Total Time**: 3 hours 20 minutes (200 minutes)
- **Physics Time**: Approximately 50-55 minutes recommended

## ðŸŽ¯ Section B Strategy (Choose 10 from 15)

### How to Choose
1. Quickly scan all 15 questions
2. Identify 10 easiest questions
3. Mark them for attempt
4. Solve only those 10

- Choose wisely, attempt only 10

## ðŸ† During NEET Exam

### Time Management for Physics (55 minutes)
- **0-5 min**: Scan all 50 physics questions
- **5-40 min**: Solve Section A (35 questions)
- **40-55 min**: Solve Section B (choose and attempt 10)
- Mark easy questions first, difficult later

### Question Attempt Strategy
1. Start with easy questions (confidence builder)
2. Then medium difficulty
3. Skip very tough questions
4. Come back if time permits
5. Don't spend >1.5 min on any question initially

### Section B Critical Advice
- Read all 15 questions carefully
- Select 10 BEFORE starting to solve
- Mark your 10 chosen questions
- Solve only those 10
- Don't change your selection mid-way
