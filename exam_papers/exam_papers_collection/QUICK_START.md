## ğŸ“š Recommended Study Sequence

### For CBSE Students
1. Complete NCERT textbooks
2. Solve NCERT examples and exercises
3. Practice previous year papers (5 years minimum)
4. Solve sample papers (current year)
5. Take mock tests

### For JEE Main Students
1. NCERT foundation
2. Reference books (HC Verma, DC Pandey for Physics)
3. Topic-wise previous year questions
4. Full-length mock tests
5. Last 5 years papers (all shifts)

### For JEE Advanced Students
1. Strong JEE Main preparation first
2. Advanced reference books (Irodov, Cengage)
3. Past 15 years JEE Advanced papers
4. IIT coaching material
5. Full-length mock tests

### For NEET Students
1. NCERT (read 5-6 times)
2. NCERT Exemplar
3. Previous year NEET papers (10 years)
4. Biology focus (360/720 marks!)
5. Regular mock tests
