## ðŸ“‹ Exam Pattern

### Paper Structure (Computer-Based Test)
- **Total Questions**: 30 (Physics section)
- **Questions to Attempt**: 25
- **Section A**: 20 questions (compulsory)
- **Section B**: 10 questions (attempt any 5)
- **Total Marks**: 100 (for Physics)
- **Duration**: 3 hours (for entire paper - PCM)

### Marking Scheme
- **Correct Answer**: +4 marks
- **Incorrect Answer**: -1 mark
- **Unattempted**: 0 marks

## ðŸ“š Important Topics with Weightage

### High Weightage Topics (8-12 marks each)

1. **Modern Physics**
   - Photoelectric effect
   - Bohr's model
   - Nuclear physics
   - Semiconductor devices

2. **Electrostatics & Capacitance**
   - Electric field and potential
   - Capacitor combinations
   - Energy stored in capacitors

3. **Current Electricity**
   - Kirchhoff's laws
   - Wheatstone bridge
   - RC circuits

4. **Magnetic Effects**
   - Biot-Savart law
   - Ampere's law
   - Force on moving charges

5. **Electromagnetic Induction**
   - Faraday's law
   - Lenz's law
   - AC circuits

6. **Optics**
   - Ray optics (mirrors, lenses)
   - Wave optics (interference, diffraction)

### Medium Weightage (4-8 marks)

7. **Rotational Motion**
   - Moment of inertia
   - Angular momentum
   - Rolling motion

8. **Thermodynamics**
   - Laws of thermodynamics
   - Heat engines
   - Carnot cycle

9. **Simple Harmonic Motion**
   - Spring mass system
   - Pendulum
   - Energy in SHM

10. **Waves & Sound**
    - Wave equation
    - Doppler effect
    - Resonance

### Standard Weightage (2-4 marks each)

11. Mechanics (Kinematics, Laws of Motion)
12. Work, Energy & Power
13. Gravitation
14. Properties of Matter
15. Communication Systems
