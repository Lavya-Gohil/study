## ğŸ“š Important Topics by Unit

### Physical Chemistry
- **Solid State**: Crystal lattices, unit cells
- **Solutions**: Colligative properties, Raoult's law
- **Electrochemistry**: Nernst equation, conductance
- **Chemical Kinetics**: Rate laws, order of reaction
- **Surface Chemistry**: Adsorption, colloids

### Inorganic Chemistry
- **d & f Block Elements**: Transition metals, lanthanoids
- **Coordination Compounds**: Nomenclature, theories
- **p-Block Elements**: Group 15-18 elements

### Organic Chemistry
- **Haloalkanes and Haloarenes**: Reactions, mechanisms
- **Alcohols, Phenols, Ethers**: Preparation, properties
- **Aldehydes and Ketones**: Nucleophilic addition
- **Carboxylic Acids**: Reactions
- **Amines**: Basicity, preparation
- **Biomolecules**: Carbohydrates, proteins, vitamins
- **Polymers**: Types, polymerization
- **Chemistry in Everyday Life**: Drugs, cleansing agents
