## üìã Exam Pattern

### Two Papers (Both Compulsory)

**Paper 1** (3 hours)
- Physics: 6-8 questions
- Chemistry: 6-8 questions  
- Mathematics: 6-8 questions

**Paper 2** (3 hours)
- Physics: 6-8 questions
- Chemistry: 6-8 questions
- Mathematics: 6-8 questions

### Question Types
1. **Single Correct MCQ**: +3 correct, -1 incorrect
2. **Multiple Correct MCQ**: +4 if all correct, 0 otherwise
3. **Numerical Type**: +4 correct, 0 incorrect (no negative)
4. **Matrix Match**: Partial marking possible
5. **Integer Type**: +3 correct, 0 incorrect

### Total Marks
- Each paper: 180-198 marks
- Physics section per paper: 60-66 marks
- Total (Both papers): ~360-396 marks

## üìö Important Topics (Physics)

### Highest Weightage Topics

#### 1. Electromagnetism (20-25% of Physics)
- **Electrostatics**: Complex charge distributions
- **Capacitors**: Network analysis, energy
- **Current Electricity**: Complex circuits, bridges
- **Magnetic Effects**: Force, torque, magnetic materials
- **Electromagnetic Induction**: Complex scenarios, AC circuits

#### 2. Mechanics (20-25%)
- **Rotational Motion**: Complex problems, coupled systems
- **Simple Harmonic Motion**: Advanced applications
- **Gravitation**: Satellite motion, orbital mechanics
- **Collision**: 2D collisions, complex scenarios
- **Center of Mass**: Multiple body systems

#### 3. Modern Physics (15-20%)
- **Photoelectric Effect**: Graph-based problems
- **Bohr Model**: Energy calculations
- **Nuclear Physics**: Decay, binding energy
- **X-rays**: Moseley's law
- **Semiconductors**: p-n junction, transistors

#### 4. Optics (10-15%)
- **Ray Optics**: Lens systems, complex arrangements
- **Wave Optics**: Interference, diffraction patterns
- **Optical Instruments**: Detailed analysis

#### 5. Thermodynamics (8-12%)
- **Heat Transfer**: Conduction, radiation
- **Laws of Thermodynamics**: Cyclic processes
- **Kinetic Theory**: Distribution of molecular speeds

#### 6. Waves & Sound (5-10%)
- **Wave Motion**: Superposition, standing waves
- **Doppler Effect**: Complex scenarios
- **String Vibrations**: Resonance

#### 7. Experimental Physics (5-8%)
- Error analysis
- Instrument readings
- Graph interpretation
- Practical applications

## üí° Solving Strategy

### For Paper 1 & Paper 2

**Time Allocation** (Physics section):
- 60-70 minutes for Physics out of 180 minutes
- ~8-10 minutes per question

**Approach**:
1. Scan all Physics questions (5 min)
2. Solve Single Correct MCQs first (easier scoring)
3. Then Numerical/Integer type (no negative marking)
4. Multiple Correct MCQs (carefully!)
5. Matrix Match (if confident)

**Skip Strategy**:
- Don't waste time on unsolvable questions
- Move to next paper section
- Return if time permits
